Granny Fran Stories are Fun

And Great Life Lessons

These stories are about five year-old Grace learning about her Grandmother Fran coming to live with them.

Being five years old and having your grandmother move in with you is an eye-opening experience. There is much to learn and Grace's amazing imagination makes the lessons so much fun.
